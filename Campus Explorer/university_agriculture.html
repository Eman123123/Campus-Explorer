About University

Welcome to UAF

The University of Agriculture Faisalabad (UAF) is Pakistan's premier agricultural university, established in 1906. It's renowned for its contributions to agricultural research, food sciences, and veterinary medicine.

Ranked #1 Agricultural University in Pakistan

Over 18,000 students enrolled

600+ faculty members

6 faculties with 64 departments
